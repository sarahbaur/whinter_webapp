Information

Whisper is a speech-to-text transcription service that uses the Hugging Face Transformers library to transcribe audio files. The service supports multiple model sizes, including Tiny, Base, and Small. The service is designed to be easy to use and can be accessed via this simple web interface. It is free to use and does not require any sign-up or registration.

This service is funded through Prof. Dr. Simone Pfenninger at the English Department of the University of Zurich. The webapplication is designed and maintained by Sarah Baur. For any questions or inqueries, please contact sarah.baur@uzh.ch.
